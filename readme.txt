
Description:-
Through this project , I would like to Facilitate the Hospitals who are in need Oxygen Cyclinders by providing them the names and addresses of Avaliable Oxygen suppliers along with calculated distances between Hospitals and Suppliers & The programme also provides the latest info regarding no of covid cases received in a day , along with no of deaths reported in a day due to COVID-19.
The program also have the capability to show latest news regarding COVID-19. 

Working :-
-> First, Program fetch the data of hospitals who are in need of Oxygen
-> On the bases of algorithm, it seprates out the name of hospitals. 
-> fetch the data of Oxygen Suppliers 
-> algorithm is applied on the data and addresses and names are sorted
-> Then it calculates the distances between each Hospital & Suppliers in Km , m , coordinates,etc
-> Give Info about no of recently reported COVID-19 cases & no Deaths reported.
-> Provides Latest news regarding pandemic due to COVID-19.
